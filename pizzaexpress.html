Welcome to Pizza Express

Pizza Express is world's leading franchise when it comes to pizza and other italian cuisine. We offer a large variety of cheesy items to choose from all across the globe. From hot n spicy carolina reaper to the classic pepporoni, we have it all.The corporation is Delaware domiciled and headquartered at the Farms Office Park in Ann Arbor, Michigan. As of 2018, it had approximately 15,000 stores, with 5,649 in the United States, 1,500 in India, and 1,249 in the United Kingdom. It has stores in over 83 countries and 5,701 cities worldwide. In 2018 Pizza Express' was inducted into the Queensland Business Leaders Hall of Fame
